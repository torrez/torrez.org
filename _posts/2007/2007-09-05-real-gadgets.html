BoingBoing Gadgets so far has been excellent. Joel's lived up to the infamous Gizmodo "spanking" and produced a great gadget blog for people who love gadgets, not just the latest overpriced black shiny thing.

Whether he's talking about retro gadgets, or devoting a more subdued amount of space to Apple's latest announcements, it's exactly the sort of gadget blog that appeals to me.
