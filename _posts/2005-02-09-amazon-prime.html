I decided to sign up for "Amazon Prime" which is Amazon's new promotion allowing people to pay a fee and receive unlimited 2-day shipping. I currently buy about one order a month from Amazon (usually in groups for free shipping), and probably buy two items at my local Borders or CompUSA. I realized that very often I don't buy things and simply add them to my wishlist where they rot and disappear.
